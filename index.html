02 · The Problem

What Happens Without TowerLink

Without a unified digital operating platform, a multi-vertical building like Axari Tower faces a cascade of operational, financial, and reputational challenges that silently erode revenue and guest experience every single day.

🏨
PROBLEM · 01

Fragmented Hotel & Apartment Bookings

Reservations are managed through phone calls, WhatsApp, and manual ledgers. Overbookings, missed reservations, and revenue leakage are common. No guest data is captured for repeat marketing.

🎬
PROBLEM · 02

No Seat Management for Cinema

Cinema ticketing relies on physical walk-ins. Seats cannot be pre-booked online, peak demand goes unmanaged, and queues deter potential customers. Revenue is uncaptured.

🕹️
PROBLEM · 03

VR Arcade Has No Digital Presence

Gaming sessions are sold on a walk-in basis only. No loyalty programmes, no time-slot booking, no digital vouchers, and no cross-promotion with other tower services.

🛒
PROBLEM · 04

Supermarket Vendors Operate Independently

Multiple vendors in the supermarket have no common digital checkout, no inventory visibility, no sales reporting for ownership, and no integration with the hotel for in-room ordering.

🍽️
PROBLEM · 05

Restaurant Orders Are Paper-Based

Food court and restaurant orders are taken manually, leading to errors, slow service, no data on best-selling items, and missed upselling opportunities for hotel guests.

🏪
PROBLEM · 06

Retail Tenant Management Is Manual

Lease agreements, rent collection, service charge levies, and communication with retail tenants are handled via email, phone, and paper — creating disputes, delays, and uncollected income.

🎪
PROBLEM · 07

Event Spaces Are Underutilised

Conference and event centre bookings are word-of-mouth. No online calendar, no self-service booking, no automated invoicing, and no ability to upsell catering, AV, or accommodation packages.

🏢
PROBLEM · 08

Office Space Occupancy Is Invisible

Hot-desking, co-working, and permanent office tenants are managed with spreadsheets. Underutilised spaces represent significant lost revenue with no real-time occupancy data.

⚡
PROBLEM · 09

Building Operations Have No Dashboard

Facility management — power, water, lifts, HVAC, security — is reactive rather than proactive. Issues are reported verbally. No centralised fault-logging, no preventive maintenance scheduling.

📊
PROBLEM · 10

Ownership Has No Financial Visibility

Axari Tower owners and investors have no real-time dashboard showing revenue, occupancy, commissions earned, or operational KPIs. Decisions are made on delayed, incomplete information.

💳
PROBLEM · 11

No Unified Guest Experience

A hotel guest cannot order from the restaurant, buy cinema tickets, or book the VR arcade from one place. Each vertical is siloed, reducing cross-selling, dwell time, and spend per guest.

📣
PROBLEM · 12

Marketing Has No Data Foundation

With no customer data captured centrally, Axari Tower cannot run targeted marketing, loyalty programmes, or personalised promotions — meaning the same guest returns without recognition.

⚠️ The Compounded Cost of Fragmentation

Each of these 12 problems individually costs revenue, time, or reputation. Together, they represent a systemic failure to monetise the full potential of Axari Tower's physical asset. Conservative estimates suggest that a building of this scale operating without a unified platform loses between 20–40% of its addressable revenue to operational inefficiency, missed upsells, and uncaptured guest spend.

03 · The Solution

What TowerLink Solves

Axari TowerLink addresses every identified problem with a purpose-built digital module. Each module works independently yet shares data across the platform — creating compounding value at every touchpoint.

SOLUTION · 01

Hotel & Apartment Management System

Real-time room inventory, online booking engine, guest digital check-in/check-out, room service ordering, and loyalty programme. Guest data is captured and marketed to automatically.

SOLUTION · 02

Cinema Ticketing & Scheduling

Online seat selection, pre-booking, QR code entry, showtime management, and concession pre-ordering. Dynamic pricing for peak vs. off-peak sessions maximises revenue per screen.

SOLUTION · 03

VR Gaming Arcade Platform

Session time-slot booking, digital vouchers, group bookings, loyalty points, and upsell packages. Integration with hotel room service for in-room voucher delivery to guests.

SOLUTION · 04

Multi-Vendor Supermarket POS & Marketplace

Unified digital checkout across all vendors, inventory dashboards per vendor, in-building delivery to hotel rooms and offices, and ownership-level sales reporting across all vendors.

SOLUTION · 05

Restaurant & Food Court Ordering

Digital table ordering via QR, in-room dining, takeaway pre-ordering, kitchen display system integration, real-time order tracking, and menu management with analytics.

SOLUTION · 06

Retail Tenant Management Portal

Digital lease management, automated rent invoicing, service charge billing, maintenance requests, complaints portal, and real-time communication between management and tenants.

SOLUTION · 07

Event & Conference Centre Booking

Online self-service booking calendar, floor plan selector, instant quotation engine, package bundling (catering + AV + accommodation), automated invoicing, and event day operations dashboard.

SOLUTION · 08

Office & Co-working Space Management

Hot-desk and private office booking, real-time occupancy display, access control integration, billing automation, and meeting room scheduling for office tenants.

SOLUTION · 09

Facilities & Building Operations Hub

Centralised fault-logging, preventive maintenance scheduling, utility monitoring, contractor management, SLA tracking, security incident log, and building health dashboard for management.

SOLUTION · 10

Owner Intelligence Dashboard

Real-time revenue, occupancy, commission earnings, tenant status, and operational KPIs in one place. Mobile-accessible. Exportable reports for investors and stakeholders.

SOLUTION · 11

Axari Guest Super-App

One app for hotel guests, visitors, and shoppers. Book rooms, order food, buy cinema tickets, play VR, shop from the supermarket, and access all tower services in a single, seamless experience.

SOLUTION · 12

CRM & Loyalty Engine

Unified customer database across all verticals. Loyalty points earned and redeemed across hotel, restaurant, cinema, arcade, and retail. Targeted SMS/email campaigns from one platform.

09 · Implementation Roadmap

From Contract to Launch

Axari TowerLink is delivered in four carefully sequenced phases, ensuring critical revenue-generating modules are live first while the full platform is built, tested, and refined over twelve months.

Phase 01 · Months 1–3
Foundation & Core Revenue Modules
Platform architecture setup, cloud infrastructure provisioning, payment gateway integration (Paystack/Flutterwave), and delivery of the highest-revenue-impact modules: Hotel & Apartment Booking System, Cinema Ticketing, Restaurant QR Ordering, and the Axari Command Dashboard (Owner View). Staff training for front desk, restaurant, and cinema teams. Beta launch with soft rollout to hotel guests.
Phase 02 · Months 4–6
Tenant & Vendor Ecosystem
Onboarding of all retail tenants and supermarket vendors onto the Axari Tenant Portal. Deployment of unified supermarket POS across all vendors. Activation of automated rent invoicing and commission deduction engine. Launch of the VR Arcade booking module and in-app digital vouchers. Full integration of the Axari Ops Console for maintenance and housekeeping teams.
Phase 03 · Months 7–9
Event, Office & Intelligence Layer
Launch of the Event & Conference Centre Booking System with self-service quotation engine. Activation of Office & Co-working Space Management. CRM & loyalty engine goes live — points earning and redemption across all verticals. Tenant analytics reports begin. Full launch of the Axari Guest Super-App on iOS and Android.
Phase 04 · Months 10–12
Advertising, Data & Optimisation
Activation of the in-app advertising platform for tenant and external brand campaigns. Launch of targeted marketing campaigns using CRM data. Advanced analytics and business intelligence reporting for ownership. System performance audit, optimisation, and documentation. Planning begins for potential white-label licensing to other properties. SLA review and platform roadmap for Year 2.

🛡️ Ongoing Support & Maintenance
